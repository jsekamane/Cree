Instructions

Please read the following instructions very carefully. It takes around 1-2 minutes to read, so please take your time.

You are paired with one other player. In each round, one of you is pick randomly and get to choose how you would like to split 100 kr. The player can choose to any split between 0 and 100 kr. After each round both players are informed about the split. Thereafter you both continue to the next round, where one of you are again picked randomly and asked to split 100 kr.

Your earnings will be the sum of all the splits allocated to you from all rounds.

Other players will not know your identity. You only be referred to as 'the other player'.
